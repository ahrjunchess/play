Set Up The Board

Arrange the chessboard with a central line dividing it into two halves, each representing victory for either the black or white side. Players are free to place their pieces on both sides.

At the beginning of the game, three black and three white pieces are initiated. This strategic positioning sets the stage for the ensuing tactical maneuvers.

Place The Pieces

Players can't randomly place chess pieces; there must be a connection.

Two types of connections: diagonal (without distance limits, even if opponent pieces are present) and horizontal/vertical (requiring an empty space next to an initial piece).

Capture Opponent's Pieces

Opponent's pieces can be captured by placing a chess piece, leaving no empty space between them.

Unlike horizontal/vertical captures (one at a time), diagonal captures require two or more connected pieces without any gaps.

Rules On How To Win

Players win by creating a connection from the center line to the board's edge. Only horizontal and vertical connections count; diagonal connections don't lead to victory.

The game concludes with a draw if moves are repeated three times, when all chess pieces are exhausted, or if all 81 grids on the board are occupied.

布局棋盘

使用国际象棋棋盘，中央线将其分为两半，分别代表黑方和白方的胜利。玩家可以在两侧任意放置棋子。

在游戏开始时，三个黑子和三个白子被初始化。这种战略定位为随后的战术行动奠定了基础。

放置棋子

玩家不能随机放置棋子，必须有连接。

两种连接方式：对角线（没有距离限制，即使对手的棋子存在）和水平/垂直（需要在初始棋子旁边留出一个空位）。

吃掉对手的棋子

通过放置棋子，可以吃掉对手的棋子，两者之间不留空隙。

与水平/垂直方式不同（一次一个），对角线方式需要连接两个或更多棋子，而且不能有间隙。

取胜规则

玩家通过从中心线连接到棋盘边缘而取胜。只有水平和垂直的连接才算胜利，对角线连接不算。

游戏以重复三次、棋子用完或者棋盘上的所有81个格子都被占满而结束。
